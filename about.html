Who Are We

Lightning Bolt Electronics is an online store that sells engineering electronic components, such as CCTV cameras, televisions, smartphones, computers, laptops, woofers, robot kits, lasers, and biometrics. Got an automation problem? We provide the solutions you need to succeed, taking a bottom-line approach to deliver the best and most effective outcomes.

Vision Statement

To be one of the leading providers of automation and electrical control system solutions globally.

Our Mission

  1. To provide quality service in both automation and electrical control solutions in design, installations, services, and maintenance.
  2. To be responsive to our clients' needs and offer cost-effective solutions coupled with high-quality service.

Core Values

  1. Client First
  2. Teamwork
  3. Passion
  4. Integrity
  5. Excellence

History

  1. 2020: Lightning Bolt Electronics was founded by James Maina in Nairobi, Kenya. The company started as an online marketplace for IoT toolkit equipment.
  2. Training Programs: Later, we expanded to offer IoT training programs, empowering staff and their families through sustainable job creation.
